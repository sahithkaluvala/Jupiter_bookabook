Donate a book

If you have any books inlcuding children's books, textbooks or senior books that you don't have space to arrange or no longer need, we can help you to reuse those books and benefit other people in our community. You can drop off the books to the shopper drug store near you, or you can send us email and make a appointment, we can pick up those books in your house.

Requirement:books should be in good condition, age of donated books is not a factor.
